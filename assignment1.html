TOM AND JERRY



1.TOM

THOMAS "TOM" CAT is a fictional character and one of the two main protagonists in Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer's series of TOM & JERRY theatrical cartoon short films.
TOM is continuously after Jerry, for whom he sets traps, many of which backfire and cause damage to him rather than Jerry

2.JERRY

JERRY is a brown house mouse, who first appeared as an unnamed mouse in the 1940 MGM animated short PUSS GETS THE BOOT.

3.SPIKE

SPIKE bulldog is the deuteragonist of the TOM & JERRY franchise. He's a gray, rough bulldog that appears in many of TOM & JERRY cartoons. He has a somewhat minor friendship with JERRY and is a formidable enemy to TOM.

4.NIBBLE

NIBBLE always seems to deliver the final blow. In The LITTLE ORPHAN, Nibbles shoots a wine bottle at Tom. in Touche, Pussy Cat! he cuts open a barrel with champagne in it and washes Tom away
